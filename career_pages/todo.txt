SIGNAL COVERAGE MAP
--------------------
  Apify job boards (existing): LinkedIn/StepStone/Xing/Cadremploi -- companies posting publicly
  career_pages (this system):  companies posting only on own /careers -- often avoid board fees
  ats_pages (this system):     Greenhouse/Lever etc. -- parallel or unique listings
  overlap = higher priority signal (same company appearing in multiple sources)
